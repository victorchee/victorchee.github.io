备份：    
`git clone https://github.com/Eastonboy99/rpi-clone3.git`
`cd rpi-clone3`
`sudo chmod +x rpi-clone3`
`sudo ./rpi-clone3 -l -v /path/to/file.img`

恢复：    
按照一般的img来安装就行了，参考
https://www.raspberrypi.org/documentation/installation/installing-images/README.md
